When can I buy tickets?

You can buy tickets online until 30 minutes before the performance starts. After that time you can only buy tickets at the venue.

How can I pay for tickets?

Tickets bought online must be paid for using a credit card and all transactions are subject to a booking fee of 1.4% plus 20p.

What are eFringers and how do they work?

An eFringer voucher can be used to buy tickets for several shows at a discounted price. For example, a "6 shows for £18" eFringer costs £18 and can be used to buy tickets for 6 different shows (£3 per show).

  • eFringer vouchers can only be bought and redeemed online (you cannot use an eFringer to buy a ticket at a box office).
  • Each eFringer can only be used to buy one ticket per performance.
  • Select the My Fringers option in "my account" to manage your eFringers.

How will I get my tickets once I have bought them?

You will receive an e-mail containing details of all tickets bought online. You can print this e-mail and take it to the venue or simply display the e-mail on a mobile device.

Can I cancel tickets?

Tickets bought using a credit card cannot be cancelled.

Tickets bought using an eFringer voucher can be cancelled until 30 minutes before the performance begins. Select the My Tickets option in "my account" to manage your tickets.
